About Bloodmoney

BLOODMONEY is a horror clicker game where desperation collides with dark humor in a deceptively pastel world. What looks like a simple “click for cash” experience quickly turns into a haunting moral test. Every dollar comes at the cost of someone else’s pain—how far are you willing to go?

What is BloodMoney

BLOODMONEY is a dark-humor clicker game developed by Shroomychrist Studios and released on August 2, 2025, on itch.io. It has quickly caught attention thanks to its short but unforgettable gameplay.

The Story: Money, Pain, and Desperation

You play as someone who desperately needs $25,000 for a life-saving surgery. Out of options, you collapse on the roadside—only to meet Harvey Harvington, a strange man with an even stranger offer:

Every click = $1.

But each click means you must hurt Harvey.

The more pain you cause, the higher the payout.

From a tickling feather to needles, hammers, scissors, matches, and knives—the choices are yours. The question isn’t just “How much can I earn?” but “How much am I willing to sacrifice my conscience for survival?”

Bloodmoney Game - Gameplay: Simple Clicks, Heavy Consequences

At its core, BLOODMONEY game uses one-button controls, but behind this simplicity lies deep tension:

Click to hurt Harvey and collect money.

Use Tab or number keys to switch between different tools.

Manage your cash carefully to hit the $25,000 target.

What makes the game special is how it builds pressure:

Harvey’s reactions grow from mild surprise to visible distress.

The soft pastel visuals hide the brutality underneath.

The haunting soundtrack and narration keep you unsettled.

Every choice is a battle between empathy and greed.

Bloodmoney is available on both Mobile and PC at https://bloodmoneygame2.github.io/. No download is required.

BLOODMONEY! Endings Explained

Your actions lead to three possible endings:

Good Ending – Stop right after reaching $25,000. You drive away with your life—and your conscience intact.

Bad Ending – Spend $20,000 on a gun and shoot Harvey. In court, Harvey testifies against you, and you’re sentenced to prison.

Normal Ending – Keep clicking past your goal, buy all tools, and mutilate Harvey. The trial focuses on “grievous bodily harm,” reflecting your cruelty.

Each ending forces you to reflect on your decisions. Did you stop when you had enough, or let greed consume you?

Why BLOODMONEY! Stands Out

  • Cute pastel visuals hide brutal gameplay.
  • Quick 30-minute playthrough that leaves a lasting impact.
  • Original soundtrack “PAID IN BLOOD” sets the chilling tone.
  • A blend of dark humor and horror keeps you both amused and disturbed.
  • Three endings encourage multiple replays and moral exploration.

BLOODMONEY! – More Than Just a Game

This isn’t just another clicker. BLOODMONEY! is a psychological experiment. It forces players to confront uncomfortable questions:

How far would you go for money?

Would you stop at survival, or keep pushing until morality breaks?

If you enjoyed unique indie horror titles like Amanda The Adventurer, Baldi’s Basics, or Buckshot Roulette, then BLOODMONEY! should be at the top of your must-play list.

Final Thoughts

BLOODMONEY! blends simulation, clicker mechanics, and psychological horror into a short but powerful package. With soft visuals masking brutal choices, and a soundtrack that lingers in your mind, it’s one of those indie experiences you won’t forget after the credits roll.
