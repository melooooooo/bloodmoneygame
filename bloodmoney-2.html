About Bloodmoney 2

Bloodmoney 2 is a sequel that cuts deeper compared to the original Bloodmoney. The game shocked players by turning a simple clicker into an emotional, guilt-tinged experience. Now, Bloodmoney 2 raises the stakes with sharper mechanics, darker choices, and even more gut-wrenching tension.

What’s New in Bloodmoney 2?

Expanded Tools of Desperation

The sequel arms you with a bigger, harsher arsenal. From unsettling gadgets to brutal methods, every upgrade feels heavier—because every dollar comes at Harvey’s expense.

A Larger Goal, Higher Pressure

This time, the financial target is higher and the clock ticks faster. Desperation bleeds into every click, pushing you to weigh morality against survival.

Dynamic Endings

Bloodmoney 2 doesn’t just stop at three conclusions—it expands into multiple branching outcomes based on how far you’re willing to go. Your path feels more personal, more consequential, and far more haunting.

Atmosphere That Hits Hard

Soft pastels collide with grim themes again, but with richer animations, layered sound design, and psychological cues that make each choice land like a punch.
