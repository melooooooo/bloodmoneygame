What is Human Expenditure Program?

Characters and Setting

Harvey Harvington

Your primary focus. Harvey cycles between hope and despair, reacting dynamically to how you feed, amuse, or numb him.

Eun-Mi

Harvey’s partner appears through messages and memories, pulling you back to the human cost behind the simulation.

The entire experience runs inside a bureaucratic software suite that reduces trauma to tabs, toggles, and payouts.
