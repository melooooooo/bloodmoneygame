What is We Become What We Behold?

We Become What We Behold is a 5‑minute social experiment where your camera shapes society. What you capture spreads—what spreads changes behavior.

Gameplay

  • Photograph moments in a small world of circles and squares.
  • Notice how only “interesting” shots go viral.
  • Watch feedback loops escalate minor conflict into chaos.

Key Features

  • Minimalist design with powerful mechanical storytelling.
  • Media bias explored through interactive systems.
  • No true win state—the lesson is the point.

FAQ

Is it really only 5 minutes?

Yes—and you’ll think about it far longer. It’s widely used in media‑literacy classes.
