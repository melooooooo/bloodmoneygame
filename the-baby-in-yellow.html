About The Baby in Yellow

The Baby in Yellow is a unique horror game. At first, it looks harmless—just a babysitting game with a wide-eyed infant. But within minutes, it flips the script into one of the most unsettling indie horror experiences online.

A Babysitting Job Gone Wrong

You play as a babysitter tasked with caring for a peculiar child. Next, you must feed them, change diapers, put them to bed. So, it all seems simple. But the longer you stay, the stranger things get. The baby’s blank stare, sudden appearances in impossible places. And then, an eerie atmosphere makes every task feel like a nightmare.

Why Players Love The Baby In Yellow Game

  • Unique Horror Twist – Everyday childcare turns terrifying, making the mundane feel menacing.
  • Creepy Yet Fun – The mix of humor and horror keeps the game playful while still spine-chilling.
  • Replay Value – Random scares and unexpected events keep each playthrough unpredictable.
  • Short but Impactful – Perfect for quick sessions, but the tension lingers long after.
