What is LoveMoney?

LoveMoney is a darkly sweet clicker-style RPG spin-off inspired by BloodMoney. You meet Harvey, a too-friendly stranger who lets you tap him for cash so you can cover a $25,000 medical bill. Every upgrade promises faster progress, but each choice tugs at your conscience.

The setup looks innocent: click to earn a dollar, buy items to raise your income, clear the bill, save your life. But every interaction with Harvey bends romance, loyalty, and ethics—asking how far you’re willing to go when money is the only lifeline.

Gameplay Overview

  • Click Harvey to earn money and unlock higher payouts with shop items.
  • Balance speed against morality as Harvey reacts to the gifts you force on him.
  • Track the mounting total toward $25,000 and decide when enough is enough.
  • Uncover branching scenes and dialogue that echo your spending habits.

How to Play LoveMoney

Start Clicking

Begin earning with simple taps - each click drops another dollar toward your $25,000 goal.

Unlock the Shop

Purchase the first upgrades, like the feather at $100, to double your cash per click and accelerate progress.

Invest Aggressively

Keep reinvesting profits into pricier tools that steadily raise your payout and uncover fresh dialogue.

Monitor Harvey's Reactions

Harvey's mood swings and new lines signal when your purchases are pushing him past his comfort zone.

Objectives and Challenges

Your goal never changes: reach $25,000 before time or temptation breaks you. LoveMoney is easy to learn, but the tension comes from deciding whether to grind slowly with safe upgrades or chase the fastest, messiest route to the finish.

Every big purchase pushes the story forward. Harvey's expressions soften, harden, or turn unsettling based on how aggressively you chase profit. The more you treat him like an object, the more the tone shifts from playful to predatory.

Upgrades and Moral Trade-Offs

Feather

The first power boost is cheap, but it hints at how personal the upgrades will get.

Luxury Items

Lavish gifts spike your earnings yet blur the lines between manipulation and affection.

Late-Game Unlocks

The final items reel in cash fast enough to finish the bill, but they demand you abandon restraint.

Each upgrade is a question: do you push Harvey further for profit, or protect what is left of your empathy?

Possible Endings

Finish the grind and you will see one of multiple conclusions. LoveMoney mirrors BloodMoney with branching finales shaped by the way you treated Harvey.

Temptation Ending

Push boundaries and you may witness Harvey betray his partner, exposing the cost of your ambition.

Restraint Ending

Stay cautious, refuse the most invasive upgrades, and the relationship remains intact even if the money trickles in slower.

Whichever path you take, the ending reveals what your hustle meant for both of you.

Tips for New Players

Upgrade Methodically

Early multipliers stack fast, so reinvest profits before pausing for story beats.

Read the Dialogue

Pay attention to Harvey's changing lines; they hint at hidden consequences and incoming twists.

Experiment With Routes

A second run with a different shopping order unlocks fresh reactions and endings.

Remember Your Motive

The core challenge is deciding how far you will go, so keep your personal line in mind with every purchase.
