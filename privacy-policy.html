Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to BloodMoney Games ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at bloodmoney.ink and play our free online horror games.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing behavior:

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type, screen resolution
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, games played, click patterns
  • Network Information: IP address (anonymized), approximate geographic location (country/region level)

2.2 Information from Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Analytics: For understanding website usage patterns and improving user experience
  • Google AdSense: For displaying relevant advertisements
  • Cloudflare: For content delivery and security services

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our gaming services
  • Improve and optimize website performance
  • Understand user preferences and gaming patterns
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Protect against malicious activity and ensure website security
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to display relevant ads

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect some features of our games.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with:

  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics)
  • Advertising partners (Google AdSense)
  • Infrastructure providers (Cloudflare)

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security monitoring through Cloudflare
  • Limited access to data on a need-to-know basis

However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website and games are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Due to the horror nature of our games, we recommend parental guidance for users under 18.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request information about the data we collect
  • Opt-out: Disable cookies or use ad blockers
  • Do Not Track: We respect browser "Do Not Track" signals

For users in the EU/EEA, you have additional rights under GDPR. For California residents, you have rights under CCPA.

9. International Data Transfers

Our website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages with global CDN distribution. Your information may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
